About WeatherOx:-

It is a weather providing website which gives you weather update of your locality(i.e where you are right now ) based on latitude and longitude.To use it ,simply click the Fetch weather button and you get the weather. Concepts I used for making this app is Async-await, API,http get,post,request,setTimeout, EventListeners & little bit of styling.
